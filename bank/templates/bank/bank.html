How it works

We're not a traditional bank, we don't give out loans, there are no interests and we don't even have any money ourselves. Instead, our goal is to provide a solid technical framework for handling money, transactions & currencies to the international community of our community.

Getting started is easy, just register with a username and password here. We don't need to know anything else about you.

Once registered, you can open as many bank accounts as you want. You can select the currency and a unique name, so you know which ones which, for every bank account that you open. All currencies are issued by third-parties, for example by governments or private groups.

Every bank account has its own, unique International Bank Account Number, the IBAN. When you want to send someone some money, you just have to ask them for their IBAN.

Feeling like the next Jeff Bezos? Go ahead and form an organization, mark it as a corporation and invite some employees. Your corporate bank accounts can be accessed by everyone in your organization. Publish your organization to the Marketplace and dominate your competition.

Take it to the next level by connecting with your Discord account. This enables real-time notifications on incoming transactions & updates on employee invitations, as well as the ability to check your balance and send money to other people directly on Discord.
